Updates to Exam delivery

With the transition back to in-person classes and activity on campus, there will be some changes in the way exams are delivered, including the return of face-to-face exams and the continued use of our online exam platform, Inspera.

Check the updates to exam delivery page to understand these changes.

International students are expected to be onshore for the compulsory study weeks, including the exam period.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-Factor Authentication is a requirement at UNSW for all students and staff. MFA provides an additional layer of security to help protect the University and your account from unauthorised access. To ensure you can access your UNSW accounts during your exam, you must have your authenticator smartphone fully charged and available on the day of your in-person or online exam.
